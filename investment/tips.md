Tips for "Pro" Markdown Write-ups
Use Blockquotes (>): As shown in the Executive Summary, this makes the high-level summary stand out visually from the technical details.

Horizontal Rules (---): These create clean visual breaks between the heavy sections of your analysis.

LaTeX Rendering: GitHub natively supports LaTeX in Markdown files. Using the double dollar signs $$ will center your formula on its own line, while single $ will keep it "inline" with your text.

Task Lists: For your KPIs, you can even use - [ ] to create checkboxes:

- [ ] Check Q2 Earnings for margin expansion
